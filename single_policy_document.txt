IT Company Policy Document
-------------------------
1. Purpose:
   This document establishes security protocols for handling sensitive client data.

2. Key Policies:
   - Ensure compliance with company guidelines.
   - Adhere to data protection policies.
   - Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information.

3. Submission Guidelines:
   - Submit all reports within 30 days from the date of the event.
   - Expenses above $500 require manager approval.

4. Anonymization Policy:
   - Personal details such as name, ID, and payment information will be anonymized.
